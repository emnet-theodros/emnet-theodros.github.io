About & Contact

Learn about AnimeStart and get in touch.

Hey! I'm Emnet, the creator of AnimeStart. I built this site because I noticed that anime has gotten really popular but there still isn't a great place for beginners to figure out where to start. Sites like MyAnimeList and Anime News Network are awesome but they can be a little overwhelming if you're brand new.

AnimeStart is meant to be simple, welcoming, and actually useful. Whether you have never watched a single episode of anime in your life or you're a fan looking for your next favorite show. Everything on this site is picked and written by me, so you're getting real recommendations and not just a random algorithm.
