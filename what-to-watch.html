What to Watch Next

Find your next favorite based on your mood or what you already love.

Based on Your Mood

If you are in a happy mood...

Try Spy x Family — a hilarious and heartwarming story about a fake family trying to keep their secrets from each other.

If you are in the mood to cry...

Try Anohana — a deeply emotional story about a group of childhood friends dealing with loss and grief.

If you are feeling pumped up...

Try Haikyuu — an incredibly exciting sports anime about a volleyball team chasing their dreams.

If you want something relaxing...

Try Laid Back Camp — a cozy and calming show about a group of girls who love camping together.

Based on What You Already Like

If you liked Demon Slayer...

Try Jujutsu Kaisen — a dark and action packed story about students who fight cursed spirits.

If you liked My Hero Academia...

Try Black Clover — another underdog story about a boy with no magic trying to become the greatest wizard.

If you liked Your Lie in April...

Try Toradora — a sweet and funny romance about two unlikely people who team up to help each other find love.

If you liked Attack on Titan...

Try Tokyo Ghoul — a dark and intense story about a boy who becomes half ghoul and struggles to find where he belongs.
