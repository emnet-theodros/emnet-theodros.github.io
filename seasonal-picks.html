Seasonal Picks

What's worth watching right now — updated every season.

📅 Spring 2025 — Editor's Pick

Dungeon Meshi (Delicious in Dungeon) Season 2

Genre: Fantasy / Comedy  |  Watch on Netflix

An adventuring party explores a dangerous dungeon and survives by cooking and eating the monsters they defeat. Sounds weird but it is one of the most creative and charming anime in years. A great pick for anyone who likes fantasy with a sense of humor.

📅 Spring 2025

The Apothecary Diaries Season 2

Genre: Mystery / Historical  |  Watch on Crunchyroll

A sharp-minded girl works in an imperial court and uses her knowledge of medicine to solve mysteries. Beautifully written with a clever main character. If you like mystery and historical settings this one is for you.

📅 Spring 2025

Dan Da Dan Season 2

Genre: Action / Supernatural / Comedy  |  Watch on Netflix

A girl who believes in ghosts and a boy who believes in aliens team up when both turn out to be real. Chaotic, funny, and packed with incredible animation. One of the most exciting newer shows out there.

📅 Winter 2025 — Still Worth Watching

Solo Leveling Season 2

Genre: Action / Fantasy  |  Watch on Crunchyroll

The weakest hunter in a world full of monsters gets a second chance and starts leveling up in ways no one expected. Super satisfying power fantasy with slick animation. Watch Season 1 first.

📅 Winter 2025 — Still Worth Watching

Bleach: Thousand-Year Blood War – The Final Arc

Genre: Action / Supernatural  |  Watch on Crunchyroll

The legendary anime Bleach returned with its final arc and it looks absolutely incredible. One of the best-animated action series airing right now. You will want to watch earlier Bleach first but it is worth it.
